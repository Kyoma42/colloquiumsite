SVKM

Shri Vile Parle Kelavani Mandal is a Public Charitable Trust, founded in 1934 that saw its inception in the wake of National Movement for Independence and hence the core ethos of the Mandal are packed with patriotic fervor and a spirit of indigenous enterprise. Over the past 80 years, the Mandal has gone from strength to strength with more than 35,000 students on its books and after originating in Mumbai, it has now spread its wings to major metro cities.

NMIMS

Accredited by NAAC with 3.59 CGPA and Grade A+ in the 3rd cycle (Mumbai Campus)With a legacy of 36 years, NMIMS has grown to being not only one of the top-10 B-schools in India but has also emerged as a multi-disciplinary University with 7 campuses at Mumbai, Shirpur, Bengaluru, Hyderabad, Indore, Navi Mumbai and Dhule and 14 constituent schools that include Management, Engineering, Pharmacy, Architecture, Commerce, Business Economics, Science, Law, Aviation, Liberal Arts, Design, Entrepreneurship & Family Business Management, Performing Arts & Distance Learning.Today more than 12000 students and 513 full time faculty members are part of India's most sought after academic community i.e. NMIMS.

MPSTME

MPSTME (Mukesh Patel School of Technology Management and Engineering) was born with the aim to offer Indian students a world-class learning experience. It offers B.Tech, M.Tech, MCA and PhD courses and one of its kind MBA(Tech) courses. MPSTME has been ranked 16th among Private Engineering Colleges in India by Outlook. Not only this but it has introduced first of its kind courses like Real Estate Management and Data Science and has won many awards for the same.
