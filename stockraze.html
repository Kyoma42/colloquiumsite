About

Student's Council's colloquium had organized an event called Stockraze in the academic year 2018-2019. It was one of the main events of colloquium. It was basically an insight into how the stock market works. At the starting of the game, the rules and regulations of the rounds were given to all. People came in a group of 3 or 4. And each group was given a fixed number of stocks and cash in hand. There were 5 companies introduced in the beginning. In the zeroth round, they could trade the stocks with the other groups by going to the validators and getting it stamped and verified. After the zeroth round, there was a news flash for round 1. They had 2 minutes to process the news and accordingly think which stocks to trade. After that, they had 7 minutes to go around and trade their stocks. If they had a deal with a group, they had to go to the validator to get it stamped and verified. There were seven rounds in all. And in the 5th round, a new company was introduced whose stocks could be bought either from the bank or the cashiers. If the groups had doubt, there were financial advisors. There were 3 financial advisors. 1st with 90 % probability of giving the right advice. 2nd with 70% probability and 3rd with 50% probability. They had their fees accordingly. After the 7th round, who had the maximum cash in hand and the stocks won. We had to put up the house full poster because the drawing hall where the event took place was full. People who participated were screaming for selling and buying the stocks. Everyone who played thoroughly enjoyed the game. It was a very successful event of colloquium.
