About

Student council's colloquium arranged a workshop for students interested in learning about the latest concept of the corporate world, full stack. This workshop was based on the concept of a full stack developer's career. Full stack developer is a person who has enough knowledge and experience in both frontend and backend development. This workshop presented an opportunity to the students to enroll themselves in different certificate courses around the world making them capable of handling every feild in a corporate world. This time colloquium took the responsibility of tackling the need of the hour. Full stack developers are in demand and colloquium made sure that those who are interested do not miss this opportunity. Hence, this workshop concluded a satisfying end to the first semester motivating us to do more!
