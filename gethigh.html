About

Students' Council's colloquium organized Get high 3.0 on 8th august. As it's first event of the year. It was a fun, walk in event in the boys lounge. It was an event before the executives of colloquium for the year 2018-2019 were decided. It was organised by the core and some of the senior executives. There were fun games like ping pong, find the pins in the rice bowl and roll the ball. And then the materials were given to the participants to make the tower according to the points earned in the games. And the one who made the tallest tower, won. People who came were very happy with the execution of the game and were enjoying thoroughly. Many people even came more than once for the event because they were having a lot of fun. Although many people tried to build the tallest tower but only one team could and they won the prize worth upto 3000. It was a great start for colloquium for the year.
