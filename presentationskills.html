About

Student council's Colloquium organized this event as its second event for the academic year 2018-19. This event consisted seminar conducted by a reputed management studies institute, T. I. M. E. A scholarly speaker was assigned by T. I. M. E to enlighten students on the making of a crisp but resourceful presentation. It also comprised of an aptitude test for students to make them understand their capabilities. At the end, the pleased audience stepped out with a certificate in their hands. Colloquium is proud to share that this event recieved the highest attendance than any seminar held before this. This event recieved enough praise and a sufficient boost to our team.
